About Us

Utsav Shah & Associates, Chartered Accountants is a professional services firm based in Mumbai, India. We offer a comprehensive range of services, including taxation services, regulatory services, transaction advisory services, financial & management consultancy services, assurance & risk advisory services and outsourcing services.

We are a cohesive team of professionals who are focused on providing our clients with high-quality services. With strong research and technical skills, we provide well-thought out and strategic solutions to the problems of our clients.

As trusted advisors, we are committed to provide value added services to our clients to help them make the sound financial decisions.
